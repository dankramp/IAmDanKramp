Procrastinating
I am procrastinating.
I have work to do.

Should type my paper
But I really don’t want to.
This is much more fun.

I’m clapping 5 times.
Then I’m clapping 7 times.
And now back to 5.

Look out the window.
There are a bunch of crop fields.
Flat Indiana.

Should I do my work?
That would be idiotic.
Papers are awful.

Carter, Rob, and Dan
They are with me in the car.
Long ride but still fun.

Now I see windmills.
Wow. Just a massive wind farm.
Stay sustainable.

That is all for now.
Thanks for reading these haikus.
Procrastinating
